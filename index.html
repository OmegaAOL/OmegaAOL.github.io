About Me

I craft software and graphics with an eye to elegance and detail. My interests span a variety of technologies and platforms, from Windows to Android to the Web. When you use my software, you will observe a touch of refinement not commonly seen elsewhere.

Here are some selected projects from my portfolio.

Cerulean for Bluesky

A desktop client for the social network Bluesky, concieved as a hobby project. Unlike other Bluesky clients, it supports tabbed browsing and detailed thread view while being lighter on resources than any other.

Diggbar

The new Digg Toolbar is the first, and so far, only third-party utility available for the new Digg platform. It displays a Digg post in a collapsible toolbar on top of its corresponding news article. It is superior in both design and functionality to the old toolbar.

Skyaeris

A collaborative recreation of the classic Skype from the ground-up, this new client is planned to support all major messaging platforms once complete.
